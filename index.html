Michał Barciś, MSc

Bio:

Michał Barciś is a PHd student in a doctoral school named Karl Popper Kolleg on Networked Autonomous Aerial Vehicles at the University of Klagenfurt. He has obtained his Master's and Bachelor's degrees in computer science at the University of Wrocław in Poland. His work is focused on applied robotics and communication.

Publications:

  2. A. Barciś, M. Barciś, and C. Bettstetter, “Robots that Sync and Swarm: A Proof of Concept in ROS 2,” presented at the IEEE International Symposium on Multi-Robot and Multi-Agent Systems, 2019.
  3. D. Snider, M. Mirvish, M. Barcis, and V. A. Tezer, “University Rover Challenge: Tutorials and Team Survey,” in Robot Operating System (ROS): The Complete Reference (Volume 3), A. Koubaa, Ed. Cham: Springer International Publishing, 2019, pp. 315–370.
