Michał Barciś, MSc

Bio:

Michał Barciś is a Senior Researcher at Technology Innovation Institute in Abu Dhabi, UAE. His main research interests are in making robots work in the real world and his hands-on attitude allows the research to come true. He is pursuing his PhD on "Information Distribution in Multi-Robot Systems" in the doctoral school named Karl Popper Kolleg on Networked Autonomous Aerial Vehicles at the University of Klagenfurt, Austria. He has obtained his Master's and Bachelor's degrees in computer science at the University of Wrocław in Poland.
